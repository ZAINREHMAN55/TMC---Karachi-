Your Gateway to Higher Learning and Excellence

At TMC University, we are dedicated to cultivating a transformative and enriching educational experience that prepares students for success in a dynamic global landscape. Explore the various facets of our university and the opportunities that await you.

Patient-Centric Approach

At TMC Hospital, we believe in a patient-centric approach to healthcare. Our medical professionals are committed to delivering personalized care tailored to individual needs. From routine check-ups to complex medical interventions, our team is here to ensure you receive the highest quality of medical attention with empathy and understanding.

Specialized Medical Services

Explore our comprehensive range of medical services designed to address a spectrum of health needs. Whether you require general medical care, specialized treatments, or surgical interventions, TMC Hospital is equipped with modern facilities and a team of skilled healthcare professionals to meet your health requirements.

Medical Specialties:

  • Cardiology: Our cardiology department specializes in heart care, offering diagnostics, preventive measures, and advanced treatment options.
  • Orthopedics: From joint replacements to sports injuries, our orthopedic specialists provide expert care to keep you moving comfortably.
  • Women's Health: Dedicated to women's well-being, our women's health services cover gynecology, obstetrics, and reproductive health.
  • Emergency Care: Our 24/7 emergency department is equipped to handle medical emergencies with urgency and efficiency.

Scheduling an appointment at TMC Hospital is convenient and easy. You can book an appointment by visiting our website and navigating to the Appointments page. Alternatively, you can contact our Appointment Desk during regular business hours for assistance. We strive to accommodate your schedule and ensure timely access to our healthcare services.

TMC Hospital is equipped with a 24/7 emergency department to handle medical emergencies. Our emergency services cover a wide range of urgent healthcare needs, ensuring immediate attention and expert care. If you have a medical emergency, please proceed to our emergency department, where our skilled medical team is ready to assist you.

At TMC Hospital, patient safety is our utmost priority. We adhere to rigorous infection control protocols to create a secure environment for our patients. Our staff follows strict hygiene practices, and we regularly sanitize and disinfect our facilities. In addition, we stay updated on the latest guidelines from health authorities to implement best practices in infection prevention, providing a safe and secure setting for your healthcare needs.

Admissions and Appointments

For information on hospital admissions, appointment scheduling, and general inquiries, visit our Admissions and Appointments page.
